# Legal Quest - 2D Top-Down Legal Education RPG

A pixel-art RPG game for learning legal concepts through interactive gameplay.

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to move your character
3. Press **E** or **Enter** to interact with NPCs
4. Learn legal concepts through dialogue choices

## Game Features

### Characters
- **Player** - Law student (you)
- **Judge Thompson** - Courthouse - Teaches judicial process, constitutional law
- **Officer Martinez** - Police Station - Teaches police interactions, criminal law
- **Attorney Chen** - Law Library - Teaches contract law, legal profession
- **Mr. Johnson** - Town Square - Teaches consumer rights, property law

### Map Locations
- Courthouse (top-left)
- Police Station (top-right)
- Law Library (bottom-left)
- Town Square (center)

### Legal Concepts Covered
- Judicial Process & Due Process
- Constitutional Law & Rights
- Police Interactions & Miranda Rights
- Criminal Law (Felonies vs Misdemeanors)
- Contract Law & Negligence
- Consumer Protection
- Property Law

## Controls
- **W/↑** - Move up
- **S/↓** - Move down  
- **A/←** - Move left
- **D/→** - Move right
- **E/Enter** - Interact

## Browser Support
- Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
